We want you! Register now!

What is going on?

Even since I was a little kid, thanks to my amazing mum, I have always celebrated my birthday with fantastic parties. The formula is simple: inviting all my friends to the country side and enjoy a lovely evening together with good food, nice music and plenty of laughs. The main feature of these parties is that all my friends are invited, new and old. However, in the past few years, having lived abroad, the tradition of bringing new friends has been lost. This year, I am turning 30, and for this occasion I want to bring everyone together! My hope is that this will be an opportunity for my lovely friends to reunite with mates they haven't seen in a while, but also to meet new awesome people. I can't wait to see you all there!

FAQ (work in progress)

What should I do now?

Register to confirm your participation! Then book your travel, and stay tuned for more updates!

Is there a dress code?

Yes, but it will be revealed later! For now, just know that it won't be formal, so feel free to come in something comfortable and fun.

What about food and drinks?

There will be (amazing) food and drinks. Use the RSVP note field for allergies or preferences!

Should I bring a gift?

No! I truly mean it when I say that your presence is what is most important to me. If you really want to do more, please consider a donation toward the far-away-friends-fund that will help cover travel costs for those coming from afar!
